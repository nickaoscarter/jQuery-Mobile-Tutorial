A few things to note:

Completely usable without your own JavaScript

The URL bar is only hidden if the page's content is longer than the device height. This is new behavior as of alpha 4, and in my opinion its a regression.

Uses HTML5 data attributes to semantically seperate markup

Each "page" should have:
header (data-role="header")
content area (data-role="header")
and footer (data-role="")

Markup Conventions: Lists

List styles are automactically applied to any <ul> or <ol> with data-role="listview"

Lists have lots of built in styling options

Nested lists will automagically use multi-page transitions -- but watch out. Large nested lists have a serious performance impact.

Image and icon support is built right in, just add an image into your list.
